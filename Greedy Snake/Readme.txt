THE GREEDY SNAKE

INTRODUCTIONS
Press "WASD" (with capitals unlocked) to move the greedy snake.
Don't let your snake touch the wall or itself.
Your score is printed on the screen and your goal is to reach 15 points. When you reach your goal, you win.
Hope you can win the game easily.

WARNING
snake.exe is avaliable only for 64-bit operation systems.
snake.cpp is successfully tested in Dev-C++.
